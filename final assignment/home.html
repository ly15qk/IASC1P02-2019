Our Company Paws is committed to finding loving and caring homes for all our rescue dogs. We offer grooming for adopted dogs at a discounted price to thank you for giving your dog a better home. Some people decide to have their dogs as pets while others are used as service dogs. Whatever the reason, dogs are very popular due to the fact that they're very loving, compassionate, smart, loyal and obedient. We hope you find the perfect dog for your home or enjoy our discounted grooming services! If you're thinking about adopting, but need to ask yourself more questions, click here!
